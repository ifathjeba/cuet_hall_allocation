How It Helps Students

Our platform simplifies hall management, saves time, and ensures every student gets proper accommodation without hassle. Authorities can monitor hall allocation efficiently while students enjoy a smooth experience.

Quick Room Allocation

Students can apply for available halls online, and rooms are allocated quickly without the need to physically meet the provost or hall authorities.

Full Transparency

All applications and allocations are visible online to students and authorities. This ensures fairness, prevents errors, and keeps everyone informed.

Student Friendly

Students can easily view available halls, apply online, and track their allocations. The interface is intuitive, reducing confusion and saving time.
