About NestMate CUET

Making hall allocation seamless, transparent, and student-friendly at CUET.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the hall allocation process for students and authorities at CUET. We strive to provide a transparent, efficient, and accessible platform for everyone to manage dormitories effectively.

Our Vision

To create a modern, digital-first campus environment where students can focus on academics and authorities can manage halls efficiently, without unnecessary physical meetings.

What We Do

Easy Hall Application

Students can apply for rooms online and choose preferred halls without visiting the provost.

Efficient Allocation

Authorities can allocate rooms based on capacity, preferences, and availability with ease.

Transparency & Monitoring

Every allocation is visible to students and authorities, ensuring a fair and accountable system.
